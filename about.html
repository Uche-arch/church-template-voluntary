We Are The Church

Demo Church is a vibrant community of believers passionate about knowing God, growing in faith, and making a lasting impact in our world. We are committed to sound teaching, heartfelt worship, meaningful fellowship, and transforming lives through the love and truth of Jesus Christ. Whether you're new to faith or looking for a place to belong, there's a seat for you here.

Our Purpose.

Our purpose is to honor God by building a Christ-centered community where lives are transformed, disciples are raised, and the Gospel is boldly proclaimed. We exist to equip believers, reach the lost, and reflect God’s love in everything we do — in the church, in our city, and beyond.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ, equip believers for purpose-driven living, and raise disciples who impact their communities and the world. We are called to shine God's light, preach the Gospel with boldness, and serve with love and excellence.

For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life. For God did not send his Son into the world to condemn the world, but in order that the world might be saved through him.

John 3:16-17 ESV

Our Values.

Identity in Christ

We believe every believer is a new creation in Christ — forgiven, loved, chosen, and called. Our identity is not defined by the world, but by who we are in Jesus. As sons and daughters of God, we walk in boldness, freedom, and purpose, knowing we are fully accepted in Him.

Church Is Family

Church is more than a building — it’s a family where everyone belongs. We laugh together, cry together, grow together, and support one another through every season of life. At Demo Church, you're not just a face in the crowd — you're family, and there's always room for you here.

Kingdom Culture

Kingdom Culture is about living with Heaven’s mindset here on Earth. We walk in love, integrity, honor, and bold faith — reflecting Christ in our actions, speech, and decisions. At Demo Church, we cultivate a culture where Jesus is King, people are valued, and the Gospel is lived out daily.

Spirit Led Life

We are called to live lives led by the Holy Spirit — walking in obedience, discernment, and power. At Demo Church, we teach and encourage believers to listen to God's voice, yield to His direction, and operate in the gifts and fruits of the Spirit daily. It's not just about moments — it's a lifestyle.


What We Believe.

We Believe in One True God.

We believe in one eternal God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — the Creator of all things. He is holy, loving, and worthy of all worship. Through Him, we live, move, and have our being. He is the same yesterday, today, and forever.

We Believe in Jesus Christ.

We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully God and fully man. He came to earth, lived a sinless life, died on the cross for our sins, and rose again in victory. Through Him, we receive salvation, forgiveness, and eternal life. He is the only way to the Father and will one day return in glory to reign forever.

We Believe in the Word of God

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. It is our guide for faith and life, revealing God’s character, promises, and purpose for humanity. Through Scripture, we are taught, corrected, and equipped to live in righteousness and truth.

We Believe in Salvation Through Jesus Christ

We believe that salvation is a gift of grace, received through faith in Jesus Christ. It is not earned by works, but freely given to all who believe and confess Him as Lord. Through His death and resurrection, we are forgiven, made new, and restored into right relationship with God.
