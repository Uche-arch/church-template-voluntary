Connect Groups

Connect Groups are the heartbeat of our church community — smaller gatherings where you can build friendships, grow in faith, and do life together. We believe church doesn’t just happen on Sundays. It happens in living rooms, cafés, and conversations that go deeper than a handshake.

Whether you're new to faith or have been walking with God for years, there's a group for you. From Bible study and prayer circles to youth hangouts and outreach teams, Connect Groups help you find belonging, spiritual support, and a place to thrive. You don’t have to do life alone — let’s grow together.
